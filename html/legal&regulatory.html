Understand the Rules & Guidelines Before Using Our Services

Legal & Regulatory

At Julian Creatives, we are committed to operating in accordance with the highest legal and ethical standards. This Legal & Regulatory section explains the framework, obligations, and compliance measures we adhere to when offering our services across branding, design, digital marketing, multimedia, and event technical support.

1. Compliance with Laws

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations of the Republic of the Philippines, including but not limited to:

  • Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) – Protecting your personal data and ensuring lawful collection, storage, and use.
  • Intellectual Property Code of the Philippines (RA 8293) – Respecting copyright, trademarks, and creative rights.
  • Consumer Protection Laws – Ensuring fairness, transparency, and honesty in service delivery.
  • Electronic Commerce Act of 2000 (RA 8792) – Governing the validity of electronic contracts and digital transactions.

2. Intellectual Property & Copyright

All content, designs, graphics, multimedia, and materials created by Julian Creatives are protected under copyright and intellectual property laws. Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution of our materials is strictly prohibited unless written permission is obtained from us.

3. Contracts and Agreements

All projects and services are governed by legally binding contracts or agreements, outlining the scope of work, payment terms, intellectual property rights, confidentiality obligations, and dispute resolution processes. Clients are encouraged to review and fully understand all agreements before entering into any engagement with us.

4. Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

Any sensitive or confidential information shared with Julian Creatives in the course of service delivery will be treated with strict confidentiality. Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) may be executed upon request to provide additional assurance of information protection.

5. Regulatory Updates

Laws and regulations may change over time. We regularly review and update our policies and practices to ensure compliance with the latest legal requirements and industry standards. Updates will be communicated through this page and other official channels.

6. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from the use of our services or agreements with Julian Creatives shall be resolved through amicable negotiations. If unresolved, disputes will be subject to the jurisdiction of the proper courts in the Philippines, in accordance with Philippine law.
